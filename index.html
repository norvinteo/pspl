Our Heritage

Since 1989, what started out as Jin's Brother Renovation Contractor (UEN: 40890000L) established by our founders, husband and wife duo, Ang Meng Hong and Sim Guek Tin, had evolved into Polishing Specialist Private Limited (UEN: 202400039Z).

Today, Aaron Ang, the son, a second generation craftsman, leads with both traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation - but our soul remains unchanged: an obsession with perfection that transforms stone and wood into work of arts.

Our Journey

1989

12 April

The Beginning

Established as Jin's Brother Renovation Contractor by our founders, husband and wife duo, Ang Meng Hong and Sim Guek Tin. The times where exceptional craftsmanship, reputed through word-of-mouth. From new built Housing and Development Board (HDB)'s terrazzo, condominium projects to landed estates.

1995

Specialization Begins

Streamlined focus to marble and parquet restoration. Start of rigorous research and testing, of machineries and chemicals, to refine the craft.

2000

Commercial Expansion

Set foot in prestigious commercial projects. Corporate lobbies, hotels, conservation shophouses and preserved British Colonial Houses. Establishing partnership with major architects, developers, main contractors, designers, stone suppliers, building managements, facility managers and housekeeping departments.

2015

Next Generation

Aaron Ang helms the family business, bringing fresh perspective while honouring traditional craftsmanship. Introduced digital documentation and modern project management.

2015

6 June

Digital Milestone

Marks a special moment when PSPL first established presence on social media Facebook. A determination to document an honourable craftwork. Visit our Facebook.

2016

Landmark Projects Begins

Completed restoration of An-Nahdhah Mosque, Chinese Swimming Club, Capital Tower, Courtyard Singapore Novena, Singapore Land Tower…

2018

Expertise Recognition

Became trusted Stone Consultant for Law Firms, Chartered Building Surveyors, Property Developers, Stone Suppliers, Defect Inspection Companies and Homeowners. Our expertise in stone defect inspection is determined to elevate industry standards.

2020

Pandemic Resilience

Despite a long halt due to the COVID lockdown creating backlogs, the company dealt with all unforeseen regulation changes and stood focus on strict timeline targets. Ensuring all projects are delivered in timeliness manner without compromising standards.

2024

1 January

New Chapter

Rebranded as Polishing Specialist Private Limited, continuing the spirit and legacy since 1989. Ready for the next generation of marble and parquet mastery.
